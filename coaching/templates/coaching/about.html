About CoachingMS

CoachingMS is a modern, fully responsive coaching management system designed to streamline the operations of coaching institutes. Our platform provides dedicated dashboards and features for Students, Teachers, and Admins, ensuring a seamless and efficient experience for all users.

How It Works

  • Role-Based Access: Students, Teachers, and Admins each have their own login and dashboard, with features tailored to their needs.
  • Student Features: Enroll in courses, track attendance and performance, download study materials, and view announcements.
  • Teacher Features: Mark attendance, upload study materials, manage assignments, communicate with students, and view analytics.
  • Admin Features: Manage teacher approvals, oversee courses, and monitor platform activity.
  • Modern UI: Enjoy a clean, card-based interface that is fully responsive and easy to navigate on any device.
  • Secure & Private: All dashboards and features are protected by role-based authentication.
